# Quick start

In 5 minutes you will have a working device: an ESP32-C3 will come online and appear in the portal.

You will need: an ESP32 board (ESP32-C3 DevKit, Super Mini, or compatible), a USB cable, PlatformIO, WiFi 2.4 GHz, and an account at [portal.idryer.org](https://portal.idryer.org/).

- [Get started in 5 minutes](01-five-minutes.md) — step-by-step walk-through to your first working example.
- [Detailed setup](99-detailed-setup.md) — full environment breakdown, dev REPL, troubleshooting before first boot.
